Directions

The Farmhouse at the Grand Colonial
86 Route 173
Hampton, NJ 08827

From Northern New Jersey and New York:

Lincoln Tunnel to New Jersey Turnpike South. New Jersey Turnpike South to Exit 14 (Newark Airport). Stay on the left and go through the toll booth to Interstate 78 West (Express Clinton). Take 78 West to Exit 12 (Jutland-Norton). Turn left at the Stop sign and continue to traffic light. At the traffic light head straight down Service Rd. Route 173 West. Continue for approximately ¼ mile and The Farmhouse is on the right hand side of the road.

From Southern New Jersey:

Garden State Parkway North to 287 North to Interstate 78 West to Exit 12 (Jutland-Norton). Turn left at the Stop sign and continue to traffic light. At the traffic light head straight down Service Rd. Route 173 West. Continue for approximately ¼ mile and The Farmhouse is on the right hand side of the road.

From Pennsylvania:

Interstate 78 East to Exit 12 (Jutland-Norton). Turn left at the Stop sign and continue to traffic light. At traffic light turn left and go over overpass of Route 78. Come to intersection of Service Rd. 173 West and Perryville Road, make a left onto Route 173 West. Continue for approximately ¼ mile and The Farmhouse is on the right hand side of the road.
